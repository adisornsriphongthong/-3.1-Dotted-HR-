The doctrine of idols is one of the most famous aspects of Bacon's

thought. Yet his claim that the idols lead to madness has gone almost

entirely unnoticed. This paper argues that Bacon's theory of idols

underlies his diagnosis of the contemporary condition as one of 'universal

madness'.
